My Calisthenics Journey

From Zero to Hero

Three years ago I couldn't do a single pull-up. Today I'm training for the front lever and one-arm pull-ups. Calisthenics changed my life not just physically but mentally as well.

This website documents my training philosophy, workout routines, and progress over time. My approach focuses on progressive overload, proper form, and consistency above all else.

My Workout Routines

Training programs I've used at different levels

Foundation Builder (Month 1-3)

Day 1: Push

  • Push-ups: 3x8-12
  • Pike push-ups: 3x6-10
  • Dips (assisted): 3x6-10
  • Plank: 3x30s

Day 2: Pull

  • Australian pull-ups: 3x8-12
  • Negative pull-ups: 3x3-5
  • Bodyweight rows: 3x8-12
  • Hanging: 3x20s

Day 3: Legs/Core

  • Squats: 3x15-20
  • Lunges: 3x10 each leg
  • Leg raises: 3x10-15
  • Superman holds: 3x30s
